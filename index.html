Segmap is a landscape image segmentation service based on Deep Convolutional Neural Networks. The aim of the project is Earth surface monitoring for detecting and addressing changes quickly and efficiently.

Use the form below to upload and segment your image. Your file should contain 3 channels (RGB) with pixel value range between 0 and 255.

  • 1 - Rain Forest
  • 2 - Agriculture
  • 3 - Habitation
  • 4 - Road
  • 5 - Water
  • 6 - Bare Ground
  • 7 - Mining
